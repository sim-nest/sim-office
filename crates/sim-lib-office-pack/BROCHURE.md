# sim-lib-office-pack

In one line: It packages a closed ledger year into review-ready office exports without sending anything live.

## What it gives you

Annual statements become a coordinated pack: a spreadsheet, a presentation, a
mail draft preview, and an archive preview. The package is built from exact
ledger statement values, so the numbers in every output stay tied to the closed
year.

## Why you will be glad

Closing work can be reviewed before it leaves the machine. The crate prepares
the documents and service actions as previews, which lets a host show the whole
annual accounts bundle to an operator before any mail, archive, or upload step
runs.

## Where it fits

It sits above the ledger close bridge and below service sites. Ledger code
supplies the statements, office codecs create the files, and hosts decide when
the previewed edits become live service actions.
