# sim-lib-mail

In one line: mail and calendar records stay useful without carrying private bodies around.

## What it gives you

`sim-lib-mail` gives the office family a compact shape for messages, events,
body previews, attendees, and attachment references. A mailbox item can be
linked, reviewed, archived, or projected without turning the message body into
ambient data.

## Why you will be glad

- Body text is reduced to short previews before it reaches errors or logs.
- Attachments travel as references, so large or private files stay at their
  source.
- Calendar events use the same document boundary as sheets, decks, reports,
  stores, and hosted sites.

## Where it fits

This crate is the local mail and calendar domain in the office family. It sits
above the document core and below Outlook or Graph adapters, giving those
placements one privacy-aware record to decode into and encode from.
