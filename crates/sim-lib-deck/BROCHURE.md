# sim-lib-deck

In one line: presentation content stays portable before it enters a slide file or hosted editor.

## What it gives you

`sim-lib-deck` gives the office family a compact presentation model with slide
titles, narrative bullets, tables, and external image references. A local deck
can be inspected, transformed, archived, or exported through the same document
boundary as the rest of the suite.

## Why you will be glad

- Slide content has a plain local shape before any vendor editor is involved.
- Tables and evidence images travel with the presentation instead of becoming
  detached notes.
- Deck documents fit the shared office store, surface, and codec boundaries.

## Where it fits

This crate is the local presentation domain in the office family. It sits above
the document core and below presentation file codecs or hosted slide sites,
giving those adapters one deck record to decode into and encode from.
