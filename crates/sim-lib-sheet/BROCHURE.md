# sim-lib-sheet

In one line: spreadsheets keep exact local values before any vendor file format enters.

## What it gives you

`sim-lib-sheet` gives the office family a small spreadsheet model with sparse
cells, exact rational numbers, formulas, and document projection. A local sheet
can be inspected and edited as SIM data before Excel, LibreOffice, or service
placements appear.

## Why you will be glad

- Rational arithmetic keeps spreadsheet totals exact.
- Cell edits travel through the shared office edit contract.
- Sheet documents use the same document boundary as reports, articles, stores,
  surfaces, and hosted sites.

## Where it fits

This crate is the local spreadsheet domain in the office family. It sits above
the document core and below file codecs or hosted spreadsheet sites, giving those
adapters one exact sheet record to decode into and encode from.
